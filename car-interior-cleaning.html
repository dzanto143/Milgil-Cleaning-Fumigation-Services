Car Interior Cleaning Services

MILGIL car interior cleaning services is there to ensure your car is clean. Keeping a clean interior is not just about making the car look good, but it also means keeping the dirt and germs away, meaning you are in a cleaner environment with fewer chances of getting ill or sick, especially during the winter months where the chances of catching the flu are much higher. We come to you. You can stay in the comfort of your home while we clean your car. If you are in the catchment area we are more than happy to give you the best car interior service in Nairobi.

What We Clean:

  • Seats (fabric/leather)
  • Carpets and mats
  • Dashboard, doors & panels
  • Windows and windshields
  • Trunk & boot area
  • Deep vacuuming & sanitization
