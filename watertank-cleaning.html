Water Tank Cleaning Services in Nairobi

At milgil cleaning and fumigation services, we recognize the importance of clean water for your health and well-being. Our water tank cleaning services are designed to ensure that your water storage tanks remain free from contaminants, providing you with safe and clean water for consumption, cooking, and everyday use. Our Approach: With a focus on quality and customer satisfaction, our experienced team utilizes advanced cleaning techniques and eco-friendly products to thoroughly clean and disinfect water tanks of all sizes and types. Whether you have a residential, commercial, or industrial water tank, we have the expertise and resources to meet your cleaning needs.

Our Services Include:

  • Underground & overhead tank cleaning
  • Plastic & concrete tank cleaning
  • Sludge and sediment removal
  • Disinfection and sanitization
  • Drainage and refilling support
